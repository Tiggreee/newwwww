# Semana 4 — Entrevistas y profesional básico

**Meta:** Respuestas cortas para HR / technical screen sin memorizar un discurso entero.

---

## Día 1 — Tell me about yourself (20 min)

### Estructura (60–90 segundos)

1. **Presente:** quién eres + qué haces ahora  
2. **Pasado:** 1–2 frases de antes (trabajo, estudios)  
3. **Futuro:** qué buscas + por qué este rol  

### Decir esto (adapta nombres y datos)

1. I'm Victor, and I'm a bootcamp student focusing on web development.
2. Before that, I [worked / studied] in [field].
3. I'm training to become a junior developer.
4. I'm looking for a role where I can learn from a team and ship real features.
5. I'm especially interested in [frontend / full-stack / etc.].

### Práctica

Graba 90 segundos. Si te trabas, para, respira, repite la frase anterior.

---

## Día 2 — Fortalezas y debilidades (15–20 min)

### Decir esto

1. My strength is that I don't give up when something is hard.
2. I'm organized — I use a calendar and daily goals.
3. I'm still improving my English, but I practice every day.
4. One area I'm working on is [testing / CSS / algorithms].
5. I'm taking steps to improve: [course / exercises / mentor].

### Regla

**Debilidad real + acción concreta.** No digas *I'm a perfectionist* sin ejemplo.

### Práctica

Fortaleza + debilidad + plan (6 frases).

---

## Día 3 — Proyecto en entrevista (20 min)

### Decir esto

1. I'll walk you through a project I built recently.
2. The goal was to [solve X for the user].
3. I used [HTML/CSS/JS, React, etc.] on the frontend.
4. The main challenge was [X], and I solved it by [Y].
5. If I had more time, I'd add [tests / auth / better mobile layout].

### Práctica

Presenta un proyecto en 2 minutos (timer). Usa `cheat-sheet-entrevistas.md`.

---

## Día 4 — Preguntas difíciles (15–20 min)

### Decir esto

1. That's a good question — let me think for a second.
2. I don't have experience with that yet, but I'm eager to learn.
3. Can you clarify what you mean by [term]?
4. I haven't faced that in production, but in my project I did something similar.
5. I can follow up with an example after the call if that's helpful.

### Práctica

Simula 3 preguntas que no sabes. Solo practica **cómo parar y comprar tiempo** con frases 1, 3 y 5.

---

## Día 5 — Cerrar la entrevista (15 min)

### Decir esto

1. Thanks for your time today — I enjoyed our conversation.
2. What are the next steps in the process?
3. Is there anything else you need from me?
4. I'm very interested in this opportunity.
5. Have a great day.

### Práctica

Cierra una entrevista imaginaria en 4 frases.

---

## Día 6 — Simulacro completo (30–40 min)

1. Tell me about yourself (90 s)  
2. Un proyecto (2 min)  
3. Una debilidad (1 min)  
4. Tus preguntas al entrevistador (2 min):

   - What does a typical day look like for this role?  
   - What do you expect from someone in the first 90 days?  
   - How does the team handle code reviews?

5. Anota en `bitacora.md` qué frase te falló.
