# Semana 4 - Entrevistas para Data Analyst

Meta: responder con claridad en HR y technical screen.

---

## Dia 1 - Tell me about yourself

Estructura (60-90 s):
1. Presente: que haces hoy
2. Pasado: contexto breve
3. Futuro: que buscas

Decir esto:
1. I'm [name], and I'm training as a junior data analyst.
2. I focus on SQL, data cleaning, and dashboard communication.
3. Before this, I worked/studied in [field].
4. I'm looking for a role where I can learn and deliver clear insights.
5. I'm especially interested in product and growth analytics.

---

## Dia 2 - Fortalezas y debilidades

1. My strength is structured thinking.
2. I stay calm when data is messy.
3. I'm still improving spoken English, and I practice daily.
4. One area I'm improving is statistical storytelling.
5. I'm working on it through weekly case practice.

---

## Dia 3 - Presentar un proyecto

1. I'll walk you through a recent analysis.
2. The goal was to understand [business question].
3. I used SQL to prepare and validate the data.
4. The key finding was [result].
5. If I had more time, I'd add [next analysis].

---

## Dia 4 - Preguntas dificiles

1. That's a great question. Let me think for a second.
2. I don't have that experience yet, but here's how I'd approach it.
3. Can you clarify the expected output?
4. In a similar case, I solved it by [method].
5. I can follow up with an example after the call.

---

## Dia 5 - Cierre de entrevista

1. Thank you for your time today.
2. I enjoyed this conversation.
3. I'm very interested in this role.
4. Is there anything else you need from me?
5. What are the next steps in the process?

---

## Checklist semana 4

- [ ] Grabacion tell me about yourself < 2 min
- [ ] Presento un caso con objetivo, metodo y hallazgo
- [ ] Cierro entrevista con seguridad y tono profesional
