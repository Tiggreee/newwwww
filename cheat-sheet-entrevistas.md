# Cheat sheet - entrevistas en ingles para Data Analyst

Respuestas cortas. Expande solo si te preguntan.

---

## Tell me about yourself (90 s)

I'm [name], and I'm training as a junior data analyst.
I focus on SQL, data cleaning, and clear reporting.
Before this, I [brief background].
I'm looking for a role where I can help the team make better decisions with data.

---

## Why do you want this job?

- I like your product and the impact of your data team.
- The role matches what I practice: SQL, metrics, and communication.
- I want to grow in a team with strong analytical standards.

---

## Why should we hire you?

- I am consistent and structured.
- I communicate clearly when requirements are ambiguous.
- I can explain technical findings to non-technical stakeholders.

---

## Greatest strength

My strength is structured problem-solving.
For example, in a recent case I cleaned the dataset, validated assumptions, and delivered a clear recommendation.

---

## Greatest weakness

One thing I'm improving is presenting in spoken English.
I'm working on it by recording short summaries after every analysis.

---

## Describe a project

The goal was to understand [business question].
I prepared and validated the data using SQL.
The main challenge was [challenge].
I solved it by [method].
The key finding was [result].
My recommendation was [action].

---

## If you do not understand a question

- Could you rephrase the question?
- Do you mean [X] metric or [Y] metric?
- Can I confirm the time range first?

---

## If you do not know the answer yet

- I haven't worked on that in production yet.
- Here's how I would approach it step by step.
- I can follow up with a concrete example.

---

## Closing

- Thank you for your time. I enjoyed this conversation.
- I'm very interested in this role.
- What are the next steps in the process?
