# Cheat sheet — entrevistas en inglés

Respuestas **cortas**. Expande solo si preguntan.

---

## Tell me about yourself (~90 s)

```
I'm [name], and I'm a bootcamp student in web development.
Before that, I [brief background — 1 sentence].
Right now I'm building projects with [stack].
I'm looking for a junior role where I can learn from a team and ship real features.
I'm especially interested in [area].
```

---

## Why do you want this job?

- I like the product / mission.
- The stack matches what I've been studying.
- I want to grow with a team, not just study alone.

**Ejemplo:**  
*I'm interested in this role because you work with React, and that's what I've been practicing in my bootcamp projects. I also like that your team seems to focus on mentorship.*

---

## Why should we hire you?

- I'm motivated and I learn fast.
- I finish projects even when they're hard.
- I communicate when I'm stuck instead of disappearing.

---

## Greatest strength

Pick **one** + **example**:

- *I'm persistent. For example, last week I spent two evenings debugging until the feature worked.*
- *I'm organized — I break tasks into small steps and track them daily.*

---

## Greatest weakness (honest + action)

Template:

```
One thing I'm improving is [X].
I'm working on it by [specific action].
```

**Ejemplo:**  
*One thing I'm improving is writing tests. I'm working on it by adding at least one test per project in my bootcamp.*

---

## Describe a project

```
The goal was …
I built … using …
The hardest part was …
I solved it by …
Next I'd add …
```

---

## Tell me about a challenge / conflict

```
Situation: …
Task: …
Action: I …
Result: …
```

**Ejemplo corto:**  
*In a group project, we disagreed on the design. I suggested we list pros and cons and vote. We picked one layout and finished on time.*

---

## Where do you see yourself in 5 years?

- *I want to be a solid mid-level developer on a product team.*
- *I'd like to deepen my skills in [frontend/backend] and maybe mentor juniors.*

---

## Salary expectations (si preguntan)

- *I'm open and flexible for an entry-level role. What's the range for this position?*
- *I'd like to learn more about the role first. Can you share the budgeted range?*

---

## Do you have questions for us?

Elige 2–3:

1. What does a typical day look like for this role?
2. What do you expect in the first 90 days?
3. How does the team do code reviews?
4. What's the biggest challenge the team is facing right now?
5. How do you support junior developers?

---

## Si no entiendes la pregunta

- Sorry, could you repeat the question?
- Do you mean [X] or [Y]?
- Can you give me an example?

---

## Si no sabes la respuesta técnica

- I haven't used that in production yet.
- In my bootcamp project I did something similar with [X].
- I'd look it up and test it in a small example first.

---

## Cierre

- Thanks for your time — I really enjoyed talking with you.
- I'm very interested in the next steps.
- What are the next steps in the process?
