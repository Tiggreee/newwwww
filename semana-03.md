# Semana 3 - Reuniones, mensajes y stakeholders

Meta: comunicarte con equipo tecnico y no tecnico de forma clara.

---

## Dia 1 - Daily update corto

1. Yesterday I analyzed [topic].
2. Today I'm validating [metric].
3. I'm blocked by missing [data field].
4. I need 15 minutes to confirm scope.
5. I'll share an update by [time].

---

## Dia 2 - Mensajes profesionales

1. Quick question when you have a minute.
2. Thanks for the context.
3. I'll review this and reply soon.
4. Could you confirm the business definition?
5. Does this summary work for your team?

---

## Dia 3 - Feedback util

1. Good catch, I'll update the query.
2. I agree with this suggestion.
3. Can we clarify this requirement?
4. I see the issue, thanks for flagging it.
5. I'll send a revised version today.

Palabras del dia:

| English | Espanol |
|---------|---------|
| requirement | requisito |
| definition | definicion |
| revise | revisar/ajustar |
| alignment | alineacion |

---

## Dia 4 - Prioridades y tiempo

1. This is my top priority today.
2. I need a bit more time to validate quality.
3. I can finish by Friday.
4. Let's postpone the optional chart.
5. First accuracy, then speed.

---

## Dia 5 - Explicar para no tecnicos

1. The main insight is simple.
2. Revenue grew, but only in one segment.
3. Returning users drove most of the growth.
4. New users stayed flat.
5. The next step is improving activation.

Practica: explica una grafica en 5 frases, sin jerga.
